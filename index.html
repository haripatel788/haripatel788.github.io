Co-Founder · 2025–Present

Novus — Fintech Startup.

I'm co-founding Novus with two partners, Rishabh Teegavarapu and Anantheshwar Elumalai. The idea came from a real gap: students at non-target schools have no structured way of breaking into high finance with roles like investment banking and private equity. The existing prep resources such as Wall Street Prep, WSO, BIWS, etc. are built for students who already have the network. Novus is built for everyone else.

The product is a LeetCode-style platform for institutional finance, with live modeling challenges, school-specific recruiting roadmaps, and an AI coach that pulls from current market conditions. We're starting at Drexel's LeBow College of Business and expanding to institutions in Philadelphia. We've done advisor conversations with people at Deutsche Bank, JPMorgan, BlackRock, and Vanguard and are in early validation with students and faculty.
